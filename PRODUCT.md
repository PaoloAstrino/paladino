# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Single-user local workstations. Specifically designed for corporate compliance officers, due diligence analysts, and investigative journalists running deep-dive network analysis offline on a personal laptop.

## Product Purpose
A local-first, offline knowledge graph system (Palantir-like) that normalizes, resolves, and traces connections between messy public procurement records, companies, and people to expose risk, fraud, frontmen, and conflict of interest.

## Brand Personality
*   **Aesthetic:** High-density, serious, terminal-adjacent.
*   **Tone:** Expert, confidential, robust, precise.
*   **Color Theme:** Linear-inspired deep charcoal and black (#010102) with lavender-blue (#5e6ad2) primary accents.

## Anti-references
*   Bright, colorful consumer SaaS templates.
*   Cream/beige/sand "AI-slop" warm backgrounds.
*   Overly rounded elements (>12px cards).
*   Low-density layout with massive spacing (wastes vertical screen estate).

## Design Principles
1.  **Local Performance First:** Speed is a feature. Visualizations and tables must load immediately.
2.  **Raw Data Integrity:** Let the data speak. Provide monospace text for identifiers (CF, CIG, IBAN) and inspectable query structures (Cypher).
3.  **Visual Connectivity:** Tracing relations should be a physical, interactive process. Click to expand, inspect drawer, follow lines.
4.  **Resolute Density:** Make maximum use of screen estate for data grids and tables, avoiding generic cards unless necessary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
*   Strict WCAG contrast checks (ensure readable light gray/white text on dark background surfaces).
*   Prefers-reduced-motion support (instant animations/transitions for fast laptop rendering).
